In 2002 XXX was a huge movie. The film grossed worldwide over 250 million dollars, and fans loved his X-games style spying. In 2005, Vin left the franchise and let Ice Cube have a go at it with XXX: State of the Union. Since then Diesel has missed it, and after one of his best friends and Fast and the Furious co-star's Paul Walker's death, Vin needed a break. He needed fun, excitement, and laughter.

Enter his return to the franchise in 'xXx: The Return of Xander Cage'. A team of bad guys have stolen a device that controls every military satellite in the world. This team isn't your average baddies, so the government call on Xander Cage and his team of friends to get the device back before they do some damage with it.

In the above interview, Toni Collette tells us why fans love the franchise so much, Nina Dobrev talks about Vin's connection to Xander Cage, Tony Jaa says what his reaction was when he found out he'd be in a xXx film, Ruby Rose discusses why people love Diesel as Xander, and Vin Diesel talks about why he came back to the franchise.

xXx: The Return of Xander Cage comes out into theaters on January 20, 2017.
