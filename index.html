\n

This packable, portable breakfast in a jar is chock-full of antioxidants, probiotics and calcium for a healthy delicious meal on the go. Oh, and it doesn’t look so bad either.

\n

What You Need:
 - 1½ cups frozen berries
\n - ¾ cup skim milk
\n - 2 cups plain yogurt
\n - 1 cup fresh berries
\n - ½ cup granola

\n

Step 1: Blend the frozen berries, skim milk and 1 cup plain yogurt in a blender until well combined.

\n

Step 2: Add the fresh berries to the bottom of a mason jar.

\n

Step 3: Layer in the granola next, followed by 1 cup plain yogurt.

\n

Step 4: Pour the smoothie in on top of the yogurt.

\n

Step 5: Finish it off with an additional sprinkle of granola and fresh fruit.
